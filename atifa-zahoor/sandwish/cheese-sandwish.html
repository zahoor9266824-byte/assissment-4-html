cheese sandwish

recpie of cheese asndwish

1. Bread ki dono slices ko ek saath rakh dein. 2. Ek slice par butter lagayein. 3. Cheese ki slice ko butter wali slice par rakh dein. 4. Dusri slice ko cheese wali slice par rakh dein, butter wali side upar ki taraf. 5. Ek toaster ya phir ek pan mein sandwich ko medium heat par 2-3 minute ke liye toast karein, ya phir until bread golden brown ho jaye aur cheese melted ho jaye. 6. Sandwich ko heat se nikal kar 1-2 minute ke liye thanda hone dein. 7. Sandwich ko half mein cut karein aur serve karein.
