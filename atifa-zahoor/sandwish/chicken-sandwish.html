chicken sandwish

recpie of chicken sandwish

1. Chicken breast ko ek pan mein medium heat par 5-6 minute ke liye cook karein, ya phir until ye gal jaye aur cooked ho jaye. 2. Chicken ko heat se nikal kar 2-3 minute ke liye thanda hone dein. 3. Chicken ko chhote tukdon mein cut karein. 4. Bread ki dono slices ko ek saath rakh dein. 5. Ek slice par mayonnaise aur mustard lagayein. 6. Chicken ke tukdon ko mayonnaise wali slice par rakh dein. 7. Lettuce, tomato, aur cheese (agar use kar rahe hain) ko chicken par rakh dein. 8. Dusri slice ko chicken wali slice par rakh dein. 9. Ek toaster ya phir ek pan mein sandwich ko medium heat par 2-3 minute ke liye toast karein, ya phir until bread golden brown ho jaye. 10. Sandwich ko heat se nikal kar 1-2 minute ke liye thanda hone dein. 11. Sandwich ko half mein cut karein aur serve karein.
