peprooni pizza

recpie of peproonipizza

1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). 2. Pizza crust ko baking sheet par rakh dein. 3. Pizza sauce ko crust par evenly spread karein. 4. Pepperoni slices ko sauce par rakh dein. 5. Mozzarella cheese ko pepperoni par sprinkle karein. 6. Chopped parsley aur basil ko cheese par sprinkle karein. 7. Salt aur pepper to taste sprinkle karein. 8. Olive oil ko crust ke edges par brush karein. 9. Pizza ko preheated oven mein 15-20 minute ke liye bake karein, ya phir until crust golden brown ho jaye aur cheese melted ho jaye. 10. Pizza ko oven se nikal kar 5 minute ke liye thanda hone dein. 11. Pizza ko slice karein aur serve karein
